## why this exists

most personal branding advice is designed for people who want to feel productive. not people who want results.

it tells you to post three times a week. to "show up authentically." to share your journey. to find your unique voice and let the algorithm handle the rest.

it doesn't tell you why none of that works if your positioning is broken.

i've spent 20+ years in marketing. i've watched executives with 50k followers lose deals to competitors no one has heard of. i've watched founders spend six months building a "content presence" and then wonder why nobody calls.

the problem was never the content. it was always the strategy underneath it.

---

## what i believe

a personal brand is not a social media presence. it's not a linkedin profile. it's not a tagline or a color palette or a weekly newsletter.

it's the answer to one question: when someone who matters to your business hears your name, what do they think you stand for?

if you don't know the answer, you don't have a brand. you have a cv with better fonts.

c-suite executives and founders with strong personal brands don't have them by accident. they have them because someone made deliberate choices about positioning, messaging, and proof — then built everything else around that foundation.

"authenticity" without that architecture is just therapy. expensive, public, inconclusive therapy.

---

## what i do

i fix positioning. i diagnose what's broken and tell you exactly what broke it.

i don't manage your linkedin. i don't write your captions. i don't coach you to "be more yourself on camera." i don't sell you a 12-week program with a private community.

i work with c-suite executives and founders who are invisible outside their own organizations — or visible for the wrong reasons — or credible in the room but unknown everywhere else.

everything is async. written documents, clear frameworks, no calls, no zoom, no "let's circle back next week." if you need someone to hold your hand through a content calendar, i'm not your person.

if you need someone to tell you the truth about why your brand isn't working — and build the architecture to fix it — that's exactly what i do.

---

## what i'm not

i don't compete with social media coaches. i don't offer ghostwriting at scale. i don't help you grow followers.

more followers don't make a stronger brand. a ceo with 500 followers who closes eur 2m in deals has a stronger personal brand than one with 50k who gets conference invitations.

the executives with the strongest personal brands didn't get there by posting more. they got there by standing for something specific that the right people actually needed.

that's the only thing worth working on.
