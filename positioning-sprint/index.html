the audit diagnoses. the sprint fixes. the audit tells you what's broken. the sprint rebuilds the foundation. many clients do both — audit first, sprint second.

a longer intake form (20 min). your background, your target audience, your goals, examples of brands you admire and don't admire, and anything specific you want addressed.

no. the whole thing is async. all communication by email. the deliverable is a written document package, not a workshop.
