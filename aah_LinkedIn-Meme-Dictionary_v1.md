## FORMAT RULES (LinkedIn-specific)
- Use real `•` character (paste it). LinkedIn does NOT render markdown bullets/dashes.
- Bullets solve the real problem: on mobile, long translations wrap and blur into the next line; the bullet anchors each entry.
- Do NOT number — implies a ranking these don't have.
- Quotes around the phrase = visual anchor + "this is the thing they say."
- Avoid Unicode bold (𝗴𝗿𝗼𝘀) — try-hard + screen readers read gibberish.
- Run through `/clean` before posting (cleared: no em dash; only banned word "synergies" was cut).

## SPARE LINES (bank, cut for length/redundancy)
- "No worries if not!" = there are worries  (strong, short, universal — re-add if you drop a weaker one)
- "Just following up!" = I have a quota  (cut: redundant with "Circling back")
- "This shouldn't take long" = it will absolutely take long  (cut: dupe of "I'll keep this brief")
- "Per my last message" = you ignored me and now I'm passive-aggressive about it
- "Would love to get your thoughts" = would love your expertise, for free
- "Let's connect and explore synergies" = I sell something, you might buy it
- "Are you the right person to speak to about this?" = I'm fishing your org chart
